Our Game

Its Story

Welcome to "Moonlight Ira", a sandbox game in which you play as an astronaut lost on an unknown and hostile planet. You'll discover a futuristic city at its center, where you'll have to survive. The environment is dangerous and challenging, so be careful!

While trying to survive, you can embark on exciting quests to help the inhabitants of this strange planet.By completing them, you'll receive "Moonies" that you can exchange for new weapons or oxygen, which represents your health bar.

Are you ready to rise to the challenge in this strange city ?

Download the game now and find out if you have what it takes to survive on a hostile planet filled with intelligent inhabitants !

Gameplay

Moonlight Ira is an action-adventure video game played in 3/4 view in an HD-2D style. In this sandbox, players are free to explore the map as they please, completing missions in any order they wish.

In this futuristic city, some characters have a strange bubble above their head. Talk to them, and they will give you a mission to complete ! By bringing them the item they wanted, you will get "Moonies", which can be exchanged for better weapons. At the beginning of your journey, you start with your bare hands, but who knows, you may end up with special weapons.

Moonlight Ira is also a cooperative experience, in which a player can invite 3 of his friends to do as they please in this space city. Filled with intelligent inhabitants capable of interacting with the player(s), you will never get bored !
